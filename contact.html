FAQ

Do babies really need 4 hours of nap time during the day?
Yes, many Indian parents are now embracing sleep training—especially gentle, no-cry methods that suit our family culture. As a trusted baby sleep coach in India, I’ve worked with hundreds of Indian families, from metros to small towns, who wanted healthier sleep for their baby without compromising emotional care. Sleep training is growing in popularity because of how effective and respectful it can be.
When should I sleep train my baby?
You can begin gentle sleep training as early as 4 to 6 months, depending on your baby’s health and developmental readiness. If your baby is older and still struggles with sleep, it’s not too late—many families schedule infant sleep training well into toddlerhood. During your consultation, we’ll determine the best time to begin for your child’s specific needs.
Do babies really learn to self-soothe without sleep training?
Some babies may eventually learn to self-soothe on their own, but many need gentle guidance to build that skill. With support from a sleep consultant for babies, your child learns to fall asleep and return to sleep more confidently. My approach is to teach this without harsh methods—just consistent, nurturing routines tailored to your baby’s personality.
How soon can we start?
You can get started right away! Once you book your baby sleep consultation, we’ll schedule your intake, assess your baby’s sleep patterns, and begin creating your customized plan. Whether you’re struggling with short naps, night wakings, or bedtime resistance, you’ll receive step-by-step guidance within days of booking.
What if my baby gets sick during the sleep training process?
If your baby falls ill during the program, we simply pause and resume once your little one is better. As your dedicated baby sleep consultant, I offer flexible support—because your baby’s health and comfort always come first. We adjust timelines if needed, ensuring the process stays stress-free and effective.
